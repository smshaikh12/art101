Dishes task list:

  1. Gather dirty dishes.
  2. Scrape off additional food from dishes into trash.
  3. Rinse dirty dishes with warm water.
  4. Add soap and water to a sponge.
  5. Scrub dirty dish with soapy sponge.
  6. Get a clean dry towel.
  7. Rub the wet dish with a clean towel to dry.
  8. Put the dishes back where they belong.
